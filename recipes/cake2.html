Khatta Meetha Dhokla

Give a sweet and tangy twist to the classic dhokla with this khatta meetha dhokla recipe. It is super soft and spongy and is topped with a flavourful tempering. You can have this delicious Gujarati delicacy for breakfast or even as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Besan
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 tbsp Suji
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • to taste Salt

Beetroot Idli

Idli is a popular south-Indian recipe and most of us like to include it in our breakfast. While you must be familiar with all the classic idli recipes, here we bring you a healthy and nutritious beetroot idli that is a must-try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Light roast semolina
  • 1 cup Curd
  • to taste Salt
  • 1/2 cup Beetroot puree
  • 1/2 inch Ginger
  • 3 Green chillies
  • 1 tbsp Cashew nuts

Salsa Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 3 Potatoes
  • 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • Salt as per taste
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 tbsp Lemon

Mango Phalsa Chaat

Mango Phalsa Chaat is ideal for the hot summers when all you need is a quick meal to nourish you from within. This simple chaat recipe is probably just the chaat recipe you need today!

Ingredients

  • 2 Nos Safeda Mangoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup Phalsa berries
  • 10 nos Mint leaves, shredded
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1/2 tsp Black Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Chaat Masala
  • 1/2 nos Lemon Juice
