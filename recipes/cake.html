Mango Sandwich

Made with just about 2-3 major ingredients, the mango sandwich is another fun way to include mango in your diet. Chill in the fridge before eating or use chilled ingredients for the best experience!

Ingredients

  • 8-10 Mango Slices (Thin)
  • 1 Bowl Cream/Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Mango puree
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom powder

Paneer Bhurji Sandwich

Paneer bhurji sandwich is a simple, easy and tasty sandwich that can be made for breakfast, evening snacks or for a light lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices Bread
  • 2 tsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Grated Garlic
  • Salt
  • A pinch of Turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/4 tsp Dry Mango powder
  • 1 medium Onion
  • 1/3 cup Paneer, crumbled

Keema Naan

Keema Naan: This is a delicious naan recipe, stuffing is prepare with mince mutton & spices. Spicy stuffing makes this keema naan complete meal, you can pair this with raita or your favorite gravy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Maida
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cups curd
  • 5-6 tbsp neutral oil
  • Water as required
  • 1 cups minced mutton
  • 1/2 cups onions ,chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder

Chiken Mayo Sandwich

Chicken Mayo Sandwich is light, easy-to-make and can win-over every heart with its soothing taste. However, the technique for preparing this recipe differs from person to person. While some like adding boiled shredded chicken in the filling, others enjoy the roasted version of it.

Ingredients

  • 4 Slices Bread
  • 200 gms Chicken Breast
  • 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  • to taste Pepper powder
  • 4-5 Leaves Lettuce
  • tbsp Butter
  • taste Salt
  • 1/2 cup Mayonnaise

Grilled Chichen Sandwich

The sandwich speaks for itself! This easy to make and healthy sandwich is made with boiled chicken along with lettuce, bell pepper and seasonings like tangy chat masala with creamy mayonnaise. Try it out in your next sandwich cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Chopped and boiled chicken
  • salt as required
  • 2 slices White bread
  • 1/4 cup Red bell pepper
  • 2 tsp Butter
  • 4 leaves Lettuce romaine
  • 3 tsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Chaat masala

Bread Rolls

Enough of deep fried bread rolls, now is the time to try a healthy yet delicious version of bread rolls. Enjoy this tawa bread roll without any guilt. Try it out!

Ingredients

  • 5 Bread slices
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1 cup Mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup Finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup Finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tbsp Pepper
  • Salt according to taste
