Palak Chicken

If you want to give the classic palak paneer a meaty twist, then this palak chicken is definitely worth a try! It is loaded with essential nutrients and vitamins and makes for a wholesome dinner meal.

Ingredients

  • 500 gms Chicken (Curry Cut)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 4 tbsp Dahi
  • to taste Salt
  • 2 cups Spinach Leaves
  • 1 tbsp Kasuri Methi

Chicken Gyro

Yogurt-marinated chicken topped on pita bread with tzatziki sauce - this chicken gryo is wholesome, refreshing can be made in just under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 120 gm Chicken thigh
  • 5 gm Thyme
  • 25 gm Paprika powder
  • 5 gm Salt
  • 5 gm Pepper
  • 100 gm Hung curd
  • 25 gm Cucumber

Chicken Tawa Boti

A quick and easy chicken recipe, this chicken tawa boti only requires stir-frying of all the ingredients on a tawa. It has a masaledar taste and can be enjoyed as an evening snack.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ginger (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Green Chillies (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Onions (diced)
  • 250 gm Chicken Breast (diced)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala

One Pot Chicken Pasta

Diced chicken is sautéed in olive oil and topped with tomato puree, cream, parsley, Italian seasoning and cooked in chicken broth. This one pot chicken pasta can be made in
just under 30 minutes and is sure to leave you drooling! Garnish it with parmesan and mozzarella cheese and indulge in its goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 500 gram Chicken (diced in cubes)
  • to taste Salt and pepper
  • 1/2 cup Onion (diced)
  • 2 Garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp Fresh parsley

Satay Chicken Curry

This recipe is for all the Satay lovers! It is made with ingredients which you will find in your pantry and it’s so simple to make at home. The rich and creamy coconut is a taste profile everyone is familiar with. The chicken is first marinated in a lovely sauce full of satay flavours and seared in that sauce for extra punch. It has got all those notes of spice, nuts, sweet and savory.

Ingredients

  • 300 Gram Chicken Breast
  • 1.2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 500 ml Coconut Milk
  • 4 tbsp Soya Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Paste
  • 5.5 tbsp Thai Red Curry Paste
  • 3 tbsp Chilli Chutney Peanut Butte
  • 3 tbsp Sriracha Sauce
  • 3 tbsp Oil

Tamil Nadu-Style Chicken Salna

Tender pieces of chicken slow cooked in a mix of flavourful spices give this curry a unique taste. This Tamil Nadu Style Chicken Salna makes for a perfect lunch or di nner meal and tastes best when served with some flaky parotta.

Ingredients

  • 500 Gram Chicken Curry Cut
  • 2 Onions (Chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (Chopped)
  • 1 inch Ginger (Chopped)
  • 4-5 Garlic Cloves
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
