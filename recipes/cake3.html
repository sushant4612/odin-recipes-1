Kesar Mango Lassi

Everyone likes to drink lassi in the summer season and if the taste of mango i.e. mango is added to your favorite lassi, then imagine how fun that drink will be. That's why today we have brought for you the recipe of Mango Lassi to beat the heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Mango Pulp
  • 2 cups Curd
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp Saffron soaked in water
  • A pinch of Green Cardamom Powder

Sharbat

The refreshing flavour of mint leaves and the sweetness of gur combine in this recipe. This easy to make sharbat will refresh you in an instant. Try it out today.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp Jaggery
  • 4-5 Mint Leaves
  • A pinch of pink salt and jeera powder
  • 1 cup Cold water
